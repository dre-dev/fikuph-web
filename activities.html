Falcon Project Opening

3 Comments

Falcon Project 10 is an annual event about technology held by the School of Information Science and Technology (SISTech) of Pelita Harapan University. Falcon Project 10 with the theme I-nspire and the tagline “Change the World” consists of a series of events such as opening, various competitions, exhibition, talk show themed with technology, escape room and closing night.

We are calling all programmers, and math enthusiast to join our competition and be shine for your future aheads. Have a great chance to talk face-to-face with the best person (IT enthusiast of course) for mentorship programme.
